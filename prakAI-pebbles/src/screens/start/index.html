Instructions

  • You will see a target shape on the screen.
  • You can observe is as much as you need to memorize it, and then tap the shape.
  • Once you tap, the single target shape will be replaced by a group of shapes.
  • Your purpose is to identify (from this group) the shape that is identical to the target shape seen in the previous screen. It will be exactly identical in every way - the same shape, size and orientation.
  • As quickly as possible, touch a shape in the group that is the same as the shape that you were shown first. There is only one correct answer.
